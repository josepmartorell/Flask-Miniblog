Crawling Through the Spiderweb

Web Scrapers 2.0 This project consists of developing scripts specialized in web scraping. Some of these programs can be accessed by clicking on the links you will find on this page at the bottom of the mechanical spider image. These programs are published on the Internet and are just part of a small army of trackers that serve as the basis for more complicated later developments. Some of the patches, that you will find in the Patch Deployment tab, have been implemented as solutions to implement in some parts of them. They are just examples, although interesting, and they largely stage the... use of posts in the development of this type of software in this miniblog, in addition to serving as a basis for access to the community as a help and way of sharing technical solutions to users as well as the challenges of creating these applications.
